Welcome to Panna mehndi design

My name is Nishi Agrawal, Co-Founder of Panna Mehndi Designs & Products. Since 2020, our brand has flourished on Instagram as @panna__mehndi_designs, earning the trust of Mehndi lovers across India and globally.

Inspired by our hometown Panna, a city known for its rich heritage and elegance, our name reflects both our roots and our creative spirit. We offer premium, skin-safe Mehndi products and professional online training, proudly serving over 5,000 happy customers and training 5,000+ students worldwide.

Our mission is to preserve the tradition of Mehndi while embracing innovationcreating not just designs, but a vibrant community of empowered artists.
